Facts about Guru Nanak Dev Ji

  • Guru Nanak Dev Ji saw service to others as serving God. At a young age, Guru Ji was given some money by Their father to start a business. However, Guru Ji chose to spend this money on feeding hungry ascetics. Guru Ji called this the true trade and started a tradition known as Langar which continues across the world today. Guru Ji taught that we should only consume once we have shared with others. The mission of a Sikh is to provide basic needs (e.g. food, shelter & freedom) to all humanity.
